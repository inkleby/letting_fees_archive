About (Read More)

Letting Fees UK is a comparison site for letting agent fees.

You can see info for each council in the explore screen, or use the stats page for the overall picture.

If you're interested in helping out, leave your details on the page for your local council.

Currently we have information on 20 councils and 1088 letting agents.

Update: Letting fees will be banned from 1st June 2019.

Read our report into letting fees or our 2017 Update.
